About Me

My name is Alexander Frummet and I am PhD Student and lecturer at the Chair for Information Science at the University of Regensburg. I have a Bachelor's degree in General and Comparative Linguistics and Information Science. After that I made my Master's Degree in Media Informatics.

For more information have a look at my CV.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the field of natural language understanding. Especially I am focussing on domain-specific conversational search and the information needs that arise during such conversations. My goal is to gain a deep understanding of the information needs in the domain of home-cooking.
